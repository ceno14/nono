Taylor Swift sets women's record for most number one albums

Taylor Swift now has more number one albums than any other female artist in history following the recent release of "Speak Now (Taylor's Version). Taylor Swift has also become the first living artist in nearly 60 years to have four albums in the top 10 at the same time. They include "Midnights," "Lover" and "Folklore." Taylor Swift had also become the first living artist to have 11 albums in the top 200 simultaneously. Last October, Taylor Swift also became the first artist ever to simultaneously to take all 10 spots on the top US song chart after the release of her tenth studio album "Midnights."

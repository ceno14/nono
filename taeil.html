Experienced A Car Accident, Taeil Will Absent NCT Nation Concert

Unpleasant news came from NCT member, Taeil. The SM Entertainment agency announced that Taeil had a car accident today, Tuesday, August 15, 2023. Through a statement released, they explained that the idol had an accident in the morning while riding a private motorbike. Taeil had a car accident while driving a motorbike on his way home after completing his schedule and was immediately rushed to the hospital for treatment. After being examined, diagnosed with fractures in the right thigh and medical staff said surgery was necessary. Currently, Taeil is undergoing a recovery period and receiving the necessary treatment in hospital before surgery. Because of this, Taeil will stop all activities including preparations for the upcoming NCT concert. The agency also confirmed that Taeil would miss NCT Nation's single concert.

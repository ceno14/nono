broken melodies (nctdream)

Singer = Nct Dream
“Broken Melodies” is a heartfelt ode to love that surpasses physical distances and endures through time. Its lyrics echo the pain of separation, the longing for connection, and a fervent resolve to reunite, encapsulating the various emotions one experiences in the face of love and longing. The song is not only a musical piece but a narrative that beautifully explores the complexities and depth of human emotions in love.
